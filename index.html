MEET THE FOUNDER

Shehzad Dobariya

Founder | AI & Robotics Analyst

❝ The subject you study doesn't define your future. Your passion does. ❞

My journey into technology began during the 9th standard with a curiosity about how machines work and how ideas become reality. Although I pursued Commerce, I continued learning Artificial Intelligence and Robotics through self-learning, experimentation and real-world projects.

This journey led me from local competitions to national platforms and eventually to representing India at Global Encounters, Dubai.

Today, through ShezBotics, my mission is to empower students with practical knowledge in AI, Robotics, Arduino, ESP32 and IoT by helping them build innovative real-world projects.
